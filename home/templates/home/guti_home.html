GUTI !

An ideal tool to "install" conjugation into yourself.

Get Used To It rather than learn!

The name of this site "GUTI !" comes from this proverb.

One of the difficulties in learning a foreign language is the verb conjugation.
How the native speakers have obtained that of their native language ?
Thet did't "learn" it. They just "get used to it". In other words, they have "installed" it naturally through countless times of experiences.

We provide you an ideal tool to simulate that.

Q&As of a verb are provided randomlly, according to the conditions you set (i.e. level of words/mode/tense). Verbs are attentivelly selected (see "List" page) to cover almost all types of conjugation.

Then all you have to do is just practicing it over and over again to "install" it into yourself. At anytime & anywhere. To your best satisfaction.

How to use:

  1. Click the flag of the language you'd like to practice.
  2. This leads you to "Set" page of the language you chose.
  3. Check conditions you'd like to practice, then click "Set" button.
  4. Go to "Practice" page by clicking "Practice" on Navigation Bar.
  5. Then, start your practice !!
  6. For the next question, just click "Question" button again.
  7. When you want to change conditions, go to "Set" page via Navigation Bar and go back to #3 above.
