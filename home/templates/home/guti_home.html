GUTI !

An ideal tool to "install" verb conjugation into yourself.

Get Used To It rather than learn!

The name of this site "GUTI !" comes from this proverb.

One of the difficulties in studying foreign languages is the verb conjugation. How the native speakers have obtained that of their native language ? They did't "learn" it. They just "get used to it". In other words, they have "installed" it naturally through countless times of experiences.

We provide you an ideal tool to simulate that.

Q&As of a verb are provided randomlly, according to the conditions you set (i.e. level of conjugation/mode/tense). Verbs are attentivelly selected (see "List" page) to cover almost all types of conjugation.

Then all you have to do is just practicing it over and over again to "install" it into yourself. At anytime & anywhere, to your best satisfaction.

How to use:

  1. Click the flag of the language you'd like to practice.
  2. Before starting practice, don't forget to check conditions you'd like to try, on the lower section of the page.
  3. Then, start your practice !!
  4. For the next question, just click "Question" button again.
  5. You can change the conditions at anytime you want, by checking/unchecking each check box.
  6. To know which types & verbs are included in each level, go to "List" page via Navigation Bar.
